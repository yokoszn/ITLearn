## 🔐 Why Digital Sovereignty Matters

In an era of increasing digital dependence, understanding **how technology works** isn't optional—it's essential. Our learning paths focus on building capability, not just collecting certifications.

**Core principles:**
- 🔑 **Independence over vendor lock-in** 
- 💡 **Understanding over memorization**
- 🛠️ **Practical skills over theoretical knowledge**
- 🤝 **Community over isolation**
