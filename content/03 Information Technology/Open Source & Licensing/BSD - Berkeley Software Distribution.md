title: BSD - Berkeley Software Distribution
---


It's a family of operating systems directly derived from Berkeley Unix. There are three major distribution, which are: OpenBSD, FreeBSD and NetBSD. Another less known distribution is DragonflyBSD which aims to be a micro kernel operating system. The BSD distribution aren't like Linux distributions, they are entirely different operating systems although they might share some components.
